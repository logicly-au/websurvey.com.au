About WebSurvey

Based in Fitzroy, Melbourne, WebSurvey is an enterprise-grade online survey service for creating all types of online surveys.

WebSurvey is operated by Strategic Data Pty Ltd and is backed by their experience in statistics, mathematics and information handling. WebSurvey partners with you to design and implement surveys that are easy to use. Our services include:

  • Social Research – with years of experience working with complex research projects and health related data collection projects, we can assist you to achieve your survey goals.
  • Statistics – our Statisticians are available to assist you with analysing your data.
  • Programming – we have a team of programmers experienced in a wide range of applications, including Java, SQL, HTML, Java Script, C, C++, Perl, Python, and PHP. We tailor our surveys and data collection projects to suit your needs.
  • Front-end Design – both a visual and usability expert, our Front-End Designer can advise you on the best layout to ensure your survey is attractive and easy to complete.
  • Project Management – our dedicated project manager will work with you to ensure your survey suits your needs.
  • Data processing – our Data Technicians have years of experience working with data entry processes, scanning, data cleansing, Optical Character Recognition (OCR) and data processing.

Our delivery model is completely service driven. There's no application to learn or software to host, and your survey can be branded to provide a transparent experience to your respondents.

With capability for quantitative and qualitative questions, adaptive logic and answer variations, WebSurvey can create online surveys that behave exactly the way you want.
