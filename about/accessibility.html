WebSurvey Accessibility

WebSurvey is committed to ensuring our website is accessibile to the widest possible audience. We actively work towards accessibility best practices so people of all abilities can use our services.

Our Surveys Compliance Level

The majority of WebSurvey's standard functionality complies with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.0 guidelines at the AA standard.
WebSurvey is able to provide fully compliant surveys where required. Please contact us for more information.
